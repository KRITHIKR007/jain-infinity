About the Event

Step Sync is a solo dance competition where participants showcase their creativity, expressions, and choreography through a stunning solo performance. This is your chance to shine on stage and demonstrate your unique dance style and talent.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Dance Styles Allowed: Indian, Bollywood, Western, or Fusion
  • Time Limit: 3-4 minutes
  • Participants must submit their final audio track one day before the event
  • Props are allowed but should not pose a safety risk
  • Song selection and costumes must be appropriate and decent
  • No gender, religion, caste, or nationality-based themes or songs are allowed

Judging Criteria

  • Expression
  • Choreography
  • Execution
  • Stage Presence
