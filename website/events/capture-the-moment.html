About the Event

Capture the Moment is a photography competition that runs throughout Infinity 2025. Document the festival's most captivating moments, showcase your artistic vision, and tell stories through your lens. From candid moments to artistic compositions, this is your chance to create visual narratives that will be remembered.

Photography Categories

  • Event Coverage
  • Portraits & People
  • Campus Architecture
  • Abstract & Artistic
  • Night Photography

Rules & Guidelines

  • Maximum 5 entries per participant
  • Photos must be taken during Infinity 2025
  • Basic editing allowed (no composites)
  • Submit in JPEG format, min 3000px on longest side
  • Include camera settings in submission
  • Model release required for portrait submissions
  • No watermarks or borders
  • Original RAW files may be requested

Technical Requirements

  • DSLR/Mirrorless/Professional cameras only
  • Smartphone entries not accepted
  • Tripods and flash allowed
  • File naming format: CategoryName_ParticipantID_ImageNumber
  • Include EXIF data
