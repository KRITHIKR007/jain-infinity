About the Event

Short Film Making is a competition where participants script, shoot, and edit a film within a given timeframe. This creative challenge tests your storytelling, cinematography, and editing skills as you craft a compelling narrative in a limited format.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time Limit: Maximum 10 minutes (including credits)
  • Team Size: Up to 10 members
  • Language: Any (must include English subtitles)
  • Video Resolution: 1080p or higher is required
  • The film must be original – using pre-recorded clips from other media is strictly prohibited
  • Soft Copy Submission: Teams must carry a pen drive with their final film on the screening day
  • Content must adhere to community guidelines (no explicit/offensive material)

Judging Criteria

  • Storytelling
  • Cinematography
  • Editing
  • Originality
  • Impact
