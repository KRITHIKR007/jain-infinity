About the Event

DroneXtreme is an adrenaline-pumping drone racing competition that tests your piloting skills through challenging obstacle courses. Navigate through gates, dodge obstacles, and race against time in this high-stakes aerial competition.

Competition Format

  • Qualification Round
  • Obstacle Course Challenge
  • Time Trial Race
  • Finals

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants must bring their own racing drones
  • Drones must meet safety specifications
  • FPV (First Person View) gear required
  • Maximum drone size: 250mm diagonal
  • Valid college ID mandatory
  • Judges' decision is final

Required Equipment

  • Racing Drone (250mm or smaller)
  • FPV Goggles
  • Radio Controller
  • Extra Batteries
  • Basic Repair Tools

Judging Criteria

  • Completion Time (40%)
  • Accuracy (30%)
  • Course Completion (20%)
  • Safety Compliance (10%)
