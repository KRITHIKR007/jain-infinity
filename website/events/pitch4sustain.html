Venue: Room 002

About the Event

PITCH4SUSTAIN is a premier pitching competition that challenges participants to develop innovative solutions for sustainable development goals. Present your groundbreaking ideas to industry experts and compete for exciting prizes.

Problem Statements

  • Good Health & Well-being
  • Decent Work & Economic Growth
  • Zero Hunger
  • Affordable & Clean Energy
  • Sustainable Cities & Communities

Event Format

10:00 AM
Registration & Team Check-in
10:30 AM
Opening Ceremony & Guidelines
11:00 AM
Pitch Presentations Begin
4:00 PM
Results & Prize Distribution

Guidelines

  • Each team gets 10 minutes for presentation
  • 5 minutes for Q&A with judges
  • Presentations must include problem statement, solution, and implementation plan
  • Teams must bring their own laptops
  • PowerPoint/PDF presentations required

Prizes

1st Prize ₹25,000
2nd Prize ₹15,000
3rd Prize ₹10,000
