About the Event

Cosmic Drift is a futuristic fashion show that challenges participants to create and showcase designs inspired by space, technology, and the future. This is your chance to bring sci-fi fashion to life and redefine the boundaries of style.

Theme Categories

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team size: 3-8 members (including models and designers)
  • Performance time: 8-10 minutes on stage
  • Designs must be original and align with the futuristic theme
  • Music track must be submitted 2 days before the event
  • Props and special effects allowed (with prior approval)
  • Sustainable materials get bonus points
  • No harmful materials or unsafe elements
  • Backstage area and basic amenities provided
