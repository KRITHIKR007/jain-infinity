Where rhythm meets unity in motion

About the Event

Nritya Vedika is a grand group dance competition that brings together teams to showcase their synchronized choreography and artistic vision. Teams can perform in various styles including folk, classical, contemporary, and fusion dance forms.

Team Requirements

  • 6-12 members per team
  • 5-7 minutes performance time
  • One team per college
  • Valid college ID mandatory

Rules & Guidelines

  • Submit music track 2 days before the event
  • Props and costumes must be arranged by teams
  • No dangerous props or fire elements allowed
  • Content must be appropriate for all audiences
  • Teams must report 2 hours before performance
  • Judges' decision will be final
