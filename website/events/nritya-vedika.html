About the Event

Nritya Vedika is a group dance competition that brings teams together to perform synchronized and energetic routines in Indo-Western styles. This event celebrates teamwork, coordination, and the power of dance as a form of expression.

Team Requirements

  • Team Size: 6-15 members
  • Dance Form: Indo-Western
  • Time Limit: 6-8 minutes
  • Props Allowed: Yes, bring your own

Rules & Guidelines

  • Final Audio Track: Must be submitted one day before the event
  • Costumes & Song Selection: Must be appropriate and adhere to cultural guidelines
  • No gender, religion, caste, or nationality-based songs or themes are allowed
  • The judge's decision is final

Judging Criteria

  • Synchronization
  • Creativity
  • Choreography
  • Stage Presence
